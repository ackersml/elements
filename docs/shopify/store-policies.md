## 1. Shipping Policy

**Made to order.** Every Elements handpan is made by hand by our makers. Instruments are not held in bulk stock, so each order is prepared for you individually.

**Dispatch time.** Your instrument is typically prepared and dispatched within **7 business days** of your order. During busy periods, or for a 432 Hz tuning request, this can extend to **up to 14 business days** — we'll tell you if that applies to your order. *(Provisional — to be confirmed against the maker's actual build + dispatch times.)*

**Delivery time.** We currently ship to **European Union** destinations. Once dispatched, delivery to the EU typically takes **5–8 business days** *(provisional — to be confirmed against real carrier transit times).* Additional regions will be added after launch.

These are estimates from our carrier, not guarantees. Customs inspection can add time and is outside our control.

**Shipping cost.** Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your destination. You'll see the exact amount before you pay.

**Import duties and taxes are included.** We ship **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)**. Any import duty and import VAT for your country is calculated and collected as a separate line at checkout, and we pay it on your behalf.

> **This means you will not be asked for additional customs charges when your instrument is delivered.** The price you pay at checkout is the price you pay in total.

**Tracking.** You'll receive tracking by email as soon as your instrument ships.

**Where we ship from.** Instruments are dispatched from our maker's workshop. The Elements Origins series is made in **China**. *(To be confirmed with the maker — see open items at the end.)*

**Damage in transit.** Every instrument is inspected and packed before dispatch. If yours arrives damaged, **photograph the packaging and the instrument before doing anything else** and contact us within **7 days** of delivery. We'll arrange a repair, replacement or refund. We can't process transit-damage claims without photos of the outer packaging.

## 2. Refund and Returns Policy

**Your right to cancel (EU/EEA customers).** If you're a consumer in the EU or EEA, you have the right to cancel your order within **14 days** of receiving your instrument, without giving a reason. To use it, tell us in writing at **hello@elementshandpans.com** within those 14 days. You then have a further 14 days to send the instrument back.

**Condition.** Instruments must come back in their original condition and original case, unmarked and showing no signs of play wear beyond what's needed to reasonably try the instrument. We may reduce your refund to reflect any loss in value caused by handling beyond that.

**Return shipping.** Unless the instrument is faulty or was sent in error, **you pay return shipping.** For a high-value instrument we strongly recommend an insured, tracked service — until it reaches us, it remains your responsibility.

**Refund timing.** We refund within **14 days** of receiving the instrument back (or of proof you've sent it), to your original payment method.

**What we refund.** The price of the instrument and the standard outbound shipping cost. Where you paid import duty and taxes at checkout, we will refund those too or assist you in reclaiming them, depending on your country's rules.

**Faulty or not as described.** If an instrument arrives faulty, damaged or materially different from its description, contact us and we'll cover return shipping and offer a repair, replacement or full refund. This is in addition to, and doesn't limit, your statutory rights.

**Tuning.** Handpans are acoustic instruments and settle with playing and temperature. Minor tuning drift is normal and isn't a fault. Every instrument is tuning-checked before dispatch and carries a **1-year warranty** — see below.

**Warranty.** Each instrument carries a **1-year warranty** against manufacturing defects. This covers workmanship and materials. It does not cover accidental damage, drops, rust from moisture exposure, or normal tuning drift.

**Custom orders.** A 432 Hz tuning request is treated as a **standard tuning option, not a bespoke/custom order.** The full 14-day right to cancel above applies to every instrument, including those tuned to 432 Hz.

## 3. Privacy Policy

**Who we are.** Elements Handpans is a trading name of **Cornerstone Devs (Michelle Ackers), 525 Adelaide St W, Toronto, ON M5V 0N7, Canada**. For any privacy question, contact **hello@elementshandpans.com**.

**What we collect.** When you buy from us: your name, email, shipping and billing address, phone number (if given), order contents, and the information needed to deliver and to clear customs. We do **not** see or store your full card details — those go directly to our payment providers.

**Why we collect it.** To take and fulfil your order, to handle customs clearance, to provide support and warranty service, and to meet our legal and accounting obligations. If you opt in, to send you marketing — and you can unsubscribe at any time.

**Who we share it with.** Only those who need it to fulfil your order:

- **Shopify** — our store and checkout platform
- **Stripe** and **PayPal** — payment processing
- **Our makers and shipping carriers** — to produce and deliver your instrument, including the customs data required to ship it
- **Avalara** — to calculate import duties and taxes at checkout

We don't sell your personal data.

**International transfers.** Because our makers, carriers and service providers are in several countries, your order data will be transferred internationally in order to fulfil your order.

**How long we keep it.** As long as needed to fulfil your order and honour the warranty, and after that for as long as tax and accounting law requires.

**Your rights.** If you're in the EU/EEA or UK, you can ask for a copy of your data, correct it, ask us to delete it, object to processing, or ask for it to be transferred. Contact **hello@elementshandpans.com** and we'll respond within one month. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

**Cookies.** We use cookies to run the store, remember your cart, and understand how the site is used. You can manage these through the cookie banner or your browser settings.

**Governing law.** These terms are governed by the laws of **the Province of Ontario, Canada**, where the business is established. If you're a consumer, you keep the protection of the mandatory laws of the country where you live.
